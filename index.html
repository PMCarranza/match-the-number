Crystal Collector

Are you ready for some mathy fun??!!

Your mission if you decide to accept it will be to match the number in blue. To do this, you will collect the crystals below each one has a randomly assigned value, but you can only figure out their value by clicking on the crystals and beginning to add to your total. Once you’ve figured out their values, use that knowledge to add up their worth to match the number in blue. This game will self-restart five seconds after you have matched or go over the number in blue.

Do not go over this number!

This is the sum of your clicks
