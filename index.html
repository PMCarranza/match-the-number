Crystal Collector

Are you ready for some mathy fun??!!

Your mission if you decide to accept it, will be to match the number in blue.

To do this, you will click the crystals, each one has a number randomly assigned but you can figure out their value by clicking on the them and beginning to add to your total.

Once you’ve figured out their values, use that knowledge to add up their worth to match the number given as target.

Enjoy the game!


This game will self-restart five seconds after you have matched the number or go over it.

Do not go over this number!

This is the sum of your clicks
